About me

Hey there!

I'm Jason Peacher-Ton! Originally a Seattlite but currently living my life in Austin.

My passion for photography began after a trip to Thailand. At the time, I thought they were extremely dope photos. They weren't. But I caught the bug, and I couldn't stop looking for compositions. Soon after that, I discovered photographers such as Nick Page and Thomas Heaton, and proceeded to binge all of their content on YouTube. The logical next step was to empty my savings to buy a ticket to Iceland during spring break of college and practice on the most photogenic landscape on earth.

Since that Iceland trip, I've taken every opportunity to empty my meager college savings and travel. So far, I've traveled to eight countries and hoping to add a lot more to that list. My most recent trips took me to 16 National Parks, including Glacier, Zion, Mt. Rainier, and Yosemite. I'm now working in a full-time position, which limits my free time but... at least I don't have to scrounge for travel money anymore.

If you've gotten this far, I appreciate that you've taken the time to check out my work! Nothing makes me happier than having someone interact with the photos that I've taken. Someday I would love to make photography a full-time job. But for now, I'll continue to practice and improve. If you want to get in touch, shoot me an email!

Thanks again, I hope you enjoy the site!
