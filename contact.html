About Me

Hey there!

I'm Jason Peacher-Ton! I'm originally from Seattle, Washington, but I am currently living my life in Austin, Texas. I'm an Ultimate Frisbee player, Formula 1 addict, and an avid gamer and reader.

My passion for photography began after a trip to Thailand. At the time, I thought they were extremely dope photos. They weren't, but I caught the bug, and I couldn't stop looking for compositions. It was only natural that my love for hiking and camping grew as well. My real education began when I discovered photographers such as Nick Page and Thomas Heaton, and proceeded to binge all of their content on YouTube. The logical next step was to empty my savings to buy a ticket to Iceland during spring break and practice on the most photogenic landscape on earth.

Since that Iceland trip, I've taken every opportunity to empty my savings and travel. So far, I've traveled to eight countries and hoping to add a lot more to that list. My most recent trips took me to 16 National Parks, including Glacier, Zion, Mt. Rainier, and Yosemite.

I appreciate that you've taken the time to check out my work! Nothing makes me happier than having someone interact with the photos that I've taken.

Thanks again, I hope you enjoy the site!
